About me

A homeschool parent who built what my kids needed

I am a homeschool parent actively teaching my kids every day. The curriculum and routines I put together have helped them grow confident, curious, and joyful learners. Seeing how well they are doing, I wanted to share the same resources with other families who want gentle structure and clear progress.

Little Oat is equal parts heart and practicality: stories, projects, and reflections blended with organized tracking so you can see growth without endless paperwork.

What you can expect

  • Friendly tone that honors kids’ imagination
  • Clear lesson guides and printable activities
  • Standards-aware planning without rigid scripts
  • Progress tracking and report making you can export anytime
