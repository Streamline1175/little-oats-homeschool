Free Homeschool Hub for Tracking and Creating Reports

Built with warmth by a homeschool family, for every homeschool family

Little Oat Learners started as a way to make our own homeschool days smoother: one cozy place to log assignments, celebrate progress, and create custom reports for individual students.

Everything runs right on your computer—no accounts, no uploads, no worries. Your family’s work stays yours, and the heart of the app stays free. Premium extras and curriculum bundles are priced gently for anyone who wants a little more structure.
