Data & Offline Storage

Last updated June 1, 2026

No internet required
OatSmart stores all of your homeschool records — learner profiles, grades, assignments, books, attendance, calendars, and reports — in a local database on your computer. This data is never sent to our servers, never synced to the cloud, and never seen by us. It belongs entirely to you.
Where data lives
All app data is stored in a SQLite database in your system's application data folder. On macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/OatSmart/. On Windows: %APPDATA%/OatSmart/. On Linux: ~/.config/OatSmart/
Backups
We strongly recommend backing up your OatSmart data folder regularly using your operating system's backup tools (Time Machine, Windows Backup, etc.) or by manually copying the folder to an external drive. The app includes a built-in export function for creating backups.
Exported files
Report cards, transcripts, and worksheets exported as PDFs are saved to a location of your choosing on your computer. These files do not contain tracking or telemetry of any kind.
License verification
The only outbound network call OatSmart makes is a one-time license check when you enter your subscription key. After verification, the app operates fully offline. No learner data is transmitted during this check.
Uninstalling
Uninstalling OatSmart does not delete your data folder. You retain full ownership of all your records. To fully remove all data, delete the app data folder manually after uninstalling.
Our commitment
We will never add cloud sync, telemetry, or any data transmission to OatSmart without explicit opt-in consent and prior notice. Your family's records are yours — full stop.
